About EGA Mentorship International

Our Mission to Empower Global Leaders

A Distinguished Global Leadership Development Initiative

EGA Mentorship International is committed to connecting and empowering 10,000 youth leaders and entrepreneurs globally, bringing together diverse changemakers, seasoned diplomats, and global experts to foster collaboration and innovation.

💡 Empowerment

Providing young leaders with the skills, knowledge, and connections they need to succeed in leadership and entrepreneurship.

🌍 Global Network

Building a community of changemakers who are connected and collaborating to address global challenges across Ghana and Africa.

✨ Positive Impact

Creating lasting change through mentorship, training, and support for sustainable development initiatives aligned with the UN SDGs.

Our Impact

  • Mentored 1,000+ young leaders across 6 countries
  • 98% satisfaction rating from program participants
  • 90+ successful projects aligned with UN SDGs
  • Growing network of 300+ experienced mentors
  • Impact across all 17 Sustainable Development Goals

Our Vision

To create a global movement of empowered young leaders who are equipped, connected, and committed to addressing the world's most pressing challenges through innovation, collaboration, and sustainable action.
